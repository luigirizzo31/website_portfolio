Kavallieri HC

Content Creation, Social Media Management, Social Media Marketing, Events Design, Promotional Material and Photography for Kavallieri Handball Club: a handball team which offers Maltese athletes the possibilities of ameliorating both their skills in the sport but also to promote the values associated with sports education.

Big Interviews: The Series is a social media campaign whose aim is that of driving traffic and generating interest in prospective players who would be willing to try the sport and join the club. This was done via friendly interviews with both the male and female's senior team players, showcasing the truth meaning of reaching the maximum level in the Maltese handball sphere.
