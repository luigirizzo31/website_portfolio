About

Who I am?

I'm Luigi and I'm a passionate digital visual designer, who meticulously crafts fresh visual identities for all kinds of brands and businesses, contemporary graphic designs (print and online), minimal web designs, strategic social media content and photography.

Having worked for both local and international clients in many sectors such as: food establishments, entertainment spaces, sport sphere, local entities and much more. With a precise eye to detail, I centre my work around minimalism and simplicity to develop all your ideas in high-quality, professional and effective designs.
