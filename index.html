About

Who I am?

I'm Luigi and this is my Creative Studio 318. Currently, I am in my last year at University of Malta, reading for a Bachelors in Fine Arts, in Digital Arts and Visual Communications. Also, in 2016, I have achieved a Diploma in Design Foundation Studies. During my time as a student, I gained experience in the design and marketing field, where I worked on intern projects (part of the University’s programme) with MUZA - The Malta National Community Art Museum, ŻFINMALTA and Teatru Salesjan. Moreover, my background includes: 7 months employment in a media and design company, social media (Facebook) management for Kavallieri Handball Club, diverse freelance work, and loads of creative projects during my days at university.
