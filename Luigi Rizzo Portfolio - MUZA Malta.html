MUŻA - Musuem Map

A user-friendly musuem map, highlighting all the features found within the Maltese National gallery's premises. The four artistic themes featured inside the museum were assigned colours in order for visitors to follow and guide them through their whole experience.

In collaboration with Chalise Bugeja, Hannah Grech Pirotta, Giulia Micallef, Nina Hess & Samuel Agius
