Don Berto Restaurant

Don Berto | Birgu

Don Berto is a culinary gem, located in Vittoriosa, which is the oldest and most popular town among the Three Cities due to its historical and cultural wealth, sitting exactly in the luxury super yacht marnia in the Grand Harbour.

Its aim is to provide the very best of hospitality by serving excellent food, in pleasant surroundings.
