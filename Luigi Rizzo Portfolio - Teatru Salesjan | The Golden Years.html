Teatru Salesjan | The Golden Years

Visual identity, campaign brand guidelines, project and creative management, social media content marketing and printed advertising material. The Golden Years is a pilot project comprising of 5 performances, aiming to decrease the chance of loneliness and isolation among the aging community.

In collaboration with Adreana Gravino, Andrew Inguanez, Chalise Bugeja, Hannah Grech Pirotta, Giulia Micallef and Francesco Vella.
